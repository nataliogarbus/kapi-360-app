## The Beginning of a Strategic Partnership
Congratulations. You have taken the most important step towards your company's digital transformation: **deciding to act**. By choosing Kapi, you haven't just hired an agency; you have initiated a partnership with a team of human experts, powered by artificial intelligence to accelerate results.

Our goal in these first 90 days is not simply to launch campaigns, but to build together the **solid foundations** upon which your business will grow.

> For those who are still evaluating taking this step, this article serves as a window into our methodology. This is how we transform uncertainty into a clear action plan and tangible results.

## Your Dedicated Digital Specialist
From day one, you will be assigned a **Dedicated Digital Specialist**, who will be your management agent and main point of contact. You can contact them directly via email, WhatsApp, phone, and through our project management platform.

Their mission is to be your strategic partner: you can schedule meetings to review progress, request assistance, and count on them to interpret metrics and make the best decisions for your project.

## Our Roadmap: A Guide Plan for the First 90 Days
> **Important Note:** This is a guide plan that represents our standard methodology. Each final action plan is 100% personalized and adjusted to the objectives, initial situation, and budget of each partner.

### **Month 1: Immersion and Foundations (Weeks 1-4)**
The first month is crucial. We don't jump into execution blindly; we immerse ourselves in your business to align every action with your commercial objectives.

*   **Week 1: Strategic Kickoff and Deep Audit.**
    *   Immersion session to understand your business, margins, and processes.
    *   We thoroughly audit your digital assets and those of your competition.
*   **Week 2: KPI Definition and Control Dashboard.**
    *   Together we define the indicators that really matter for your profitability.
    *   We configure and give you access to a real-time dashboard for total transparency.
*   **Week 3: Detailed Action Plan and Base Optimizations.**
    *   We present the detailed action plan for the quarter.
    *   We perform the first technical optimizations on your website.
*   **Week 4: Foundational Content Creation.**
    *   We develop the first pieces of strategic content that will serve as a pillar for campaigns.

### **Month 2: Implementation and First Results (Weeks 5-8)**
With a clear strategy, the second month focuses on execution and obtaining the first measurable data.

*   **Weeks 5-6: Campaign Launch and First Data.**
    *   We launch the first campaigns (SEO, Advertising, Prospecting) in a controlled manner.
    *   We start seeing the first leads, ranking improvements, and performance data.
*   **Weeks 7-8: "Quick Wins" Analysis and First Optimization.**
    *   We analyze initial data to identify what is working best and double down on impact.

### **Month 3: Optimization and Sustainable Growth (Weeks 9-12)**
The third month marks the beginning of our continuous improvement cycle, focused on ROI.

*   **Weeks 9-10: Data-Driven Optimization.**
    *   We use collected data to refine campaigns, improve segmentation, and optimize investment.
*   **Week 11: Quarterly Strategic Review Meeting.**
    *   We analyze the results of the first 90 days against the KPIs we defined.
*   **Week 12: Next Quarter Planning.**
    *   With the learnings from this first cycle, we co-create the action plan for the next 90 days.
